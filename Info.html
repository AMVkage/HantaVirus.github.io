Basic information, What is the hantavirus?


The hantavirus is a virus that spreads it self through rodents.
People get infected by it when they come in contact with their urine or if humans
breath in the air that contains the Hantavirus that comes from the urine of rodents.
The virus can be life-treathening when not threated well.
It has a 33 procent death rate. However the chance of
getting infected by the virus is low.

Where does it appear?


The hantavirus appears in places where rodents are.
It can appear everywhere where rodents are.
There are no specific places it appears the most.
However most cases of the Hantavirus are west from the Mississippi River.
